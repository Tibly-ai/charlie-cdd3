Selected Work

Three photos that get brought up a lot.

01

The Ridge, First Light

Taken on the Sawtooth Ridge trail at mile six, right as the sun cleared the far peaks. Charlie had already scouted the spot twenty feet ahead and just sat down to wait for the camera to catch up.

02

The Log Crossing

A washed out footbridge on the Elk Creek loop, replaced by a fallen pine. Charlie walked it in three seconds flat while the humans took the long way through the shallows.

03

Snowed In, Still Smiling

An early October storm dumped six inches on the pass overnight. Most dogs would've turned back at the car. This one rolled in it for ten straight minutes before we got a step in.
