Meet the Dog

A blue heeler mix with a serious trail habit

Charlie showed up at the shelter in 2020 with burrs in his coat and about six months old. Four years and roughly four hundred trail miles later, he's the reason we hike every weekend instead of just some of them.

He knows the difference between a real hike and a walk around the block, and he lets us know it at the front door. Scroll through his gallery, read about his bigger trips, or come find him on Saturday mornings.
